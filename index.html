About Us

Alarming Ladder Studios is a small indie games company based in Suffolk, dedicated to creating new, engaging experiences for all.

Set up by a group of University of Suffolk graduates with extensive experience creating games, our studio aims to provide new, interesting experiences for players to dive into.

Currently, we are working on our award winning, action-packed, fitness VR game: Kingdom Run VR

Our Games

Our Studio is currently focused on the release of Kingdom Run VR, our action-packed, fitness VR experience, on SteamVR and the Meta Quest store. Along with this, we have additional planned projects in development that we are excited to provide more details for in the near future!

Latest Updates

Alarming Ladders x Games Innovation Challenge - 07/04/2022

Alarming Ladder Studios has won the Gameplay Award at the Games Innovation Challenge! Using a prototype for our project Kingdom Run VR, the judges highlighted its ‘easy-to-grasp concept paired with high entertainment value’. Alarming Ladder Studios now plans to build off the success of the Gameplay Award with a focus on work on Kingdom Run VR’s prototype.

Alarming Ladders x Dundee Pitching Event - 09/08/2022

Alarming Ladder Studios travels to Scotland. The dev team had a great time socialising with the other Tranzfuser 2022 teams before preparing for a pitching event in one of the hearts of the UK Games Industry, Dundee. Alarming Ladder Studios hopes to attend more events in the future while still focusing on completing our playable build of Kingdom Run VR for our upcoming showcase event!

Alarming Ladders x Tranzfuser - 20/06/2022

Alarming Ladder Studios was very fortunate to be accepted into the Tranzfuser 2022 programme representing the University of Suffolk. With help from the UK Games Talent and Finance team, Alarming Ladder Studios hopes to focus on creating a playable build of Kingdom Run VR for a future showcase event. This opportunity also helps us grow as a company building an ethical work culture and having access to many individuals within the games industry, including Julia Le Gallo producer extraordinaire.

Alarming Ladders x Barclays Games Frenzy - 01/07/2022

Alarming Ladder Studios friends at Barclays Games and Esports invited the team to showcase Kingdom Run VR at the Barclays Games Frenzy at Barclays headquarters in Canary Wharf. The team had a great time showcasing the project for the first time to the public seeing some amazing runs first-hand! With the feedback and responses collected Alarming Ladder Studios' goal is to continue work on the prototype for Tranzfuser 2022 and a future release (date unconfirmed).

Alarming Ladders x EGX - 26/09/2022

Alarming Ladder Studios had the amazing opportunity to promote and showcase our title: Kingdom Run VR at EGX London 2022! We had over 350 people try Kingdom Run VR within the Tranzfuser booth setting new high scores and leaving some amazing feedback. Over 50,000 gems were collected over the 4-day period with some dedicated fans coming back multiple times to beat their score. Also thanks to Barclays Games and Esports, Alarming Ladder Studios also had a chance to talk about the project's progress and goals on stage! Afterwards, we even had some brave individuals play the game on stage.

Alarming Ladders x 2023 - 12/11/2022

Alarming Ladder Studios has rested post-EGX and are hard at work! The dev team has grown and our vision is with it! Our main news is that Kingdom Run VR will be launching in early 2023 on a VR headset near you! Alarming Ladder Studios continues to attend local events and looks forward to growing and releasing multiple titles in 2023 while working with the University of Suffolk and other bodies to support new developers jumping into the industry for the first time.
